Theme: Natural Warmth

Theme designs for Timothy Bohman, Urias Miranda, and Jamie Stott. BYU-I - WDD 331R - Winter Term 2023.

For our theme, we were inspired by nature, specifically, clay and/or red rock.
To keep things warm, we have the background a soft, creamy color, rather than stark white.
For contrast, we added blues to represent the sky, and to enhance the orange color of the clay.
For the standard text color, we added warmth by using grey, as opposed to harsh black.
We created multiple headline style options that we can play with, and pick our favorite combinations as we progress through the assignment.
For the headline font, we chose a "worn" looking serif font that reminded us of the organic look we were trying to achieve.
For the body font, we chose a contrasting, yet simple, sans-serif font that is easy to read.
